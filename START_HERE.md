## To Run the Application:

Open your terminal in this directory and run:

```bash
npm run dev
```

The application will automatically open in your browser at `http://localhost:3000`

## What You'll See:

### 1. Landing Page (/)
- Beautiful glassmorphism design
- Two interactive cards:
  - **Tactical Board** - Click to build lineups
  - **Official Draw** - Click to simulate WC 2026 draw

### 2. Tactical Board (/lineup)
- Drag players around the pitch
- Change formations (4-3-3, 4-4-2, 3-5-2, 4-2-3-1)
- Auto-saves your lineup
- Reset positions anytime

### 3. Official Draw (/draw)
- Step 1: View the 4 pots (48 teams)
- Step 2: Click "Simulate Draw"
- Step 3: See the 12 groups with confederation colors
